Traditional news portals often struggle with content synchronization and manual management overhead, leading to stale information and poor user engagement. To address these challenges, this project presents NewsTV19, a comprehensive full-stack news aggregation and management system designed to deliver live, categorized content across multiple domains such as Politics, Business, and Technology. The primary objective was to engineer a platform that balances high-speed content delivery with automated data retrieval to ensure that users are presented with the most current headlines without manual intervention.The proposed architecture is built upon the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), utilizing a modular design to ensure scalability and ease of maintenance. A core technical innovation of the system is its dual-tier data fetching strategy, which employs a local MongoDB database for rapid response times and an automated RSS-based proxy server for real-time content fallback. The frontend, developed using React and Vite, features a dynamic UI/UX that incorporates advanced state management and responsive design principles. Furthermore, a robust administrative ecosystem was implemented to provide real-time team synchronization, dynamic category management, and secure role-based access control, allowing for centralized oversight of the entire news pipeline.The final implementation of NewsTV19 demonstrates a production-ready solution that effectively bridges the gap between raw data ingestion and a polished consumer experience. By integrating specialized features such as real-time view tracking, custom image scraping, and live news tickers, the platform showcases a significant improvement in content management efficiency compared to traditional manual systems. This project highlights the effectiveness of modern full-stack methodologies in solving complex engineering problems related to data consistency, real-time synchronization, and high-concurrency web environments, providing a scalable blueprint for future developments in digital journalism technology.iChapter No.TitlePage No.Abstracti1Introduction11.1Overview of the Internship11.2Objectives of the Internship11.3Scope of the Internship22Company Profile32.1Company Name & Address32.2Nature of Business & Core Services32.3Technologies / Tools Used in the Company43Internship Work Description53.1Department / Team Assigned53.2Projects / Tasks Assigned53.2.1Phase 1 — Backend Fundamentals Training53.2.2Phase 2 — Independent Project: TaskOps53.2.3Phase 3 — Company Product Contribution: TV19 News63.2.4      Phase 5 — Revora63.3Learning Outcomes73.4Tools, Technologies & Frameworks Used7-84Gantt Chart (Project Timeline)95Weekly Progress Summary10-116Future Work & Next Steps126.1Upcoming Project Phases / Tasks126.2Areas for Further Improvement10 137Conclusion147.1Summary of Key Learnings147.2Impact of the Internship on Career Plans14Chapter 1: Introduction1.1 Overview of the InternshipThis internship was undertaken as part of an industry exposure program, providing hands-on experience in full-stack web development within a professional software development environment.
