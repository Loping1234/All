- Each `index.html` includes a source-and-terms comment.
- No required attribution was removed. The approved QA rules state that these three templates do not require a visible backlink.
- The 15 A.D. Bakery page is custom HTML/CSS. No template code, zip, or package was copied for this fourth demo.
