### Rigveda' / Barista Cafe

- Retains the warm coffee palette, layered rounded cards, substantial cafe hierarchy, dark feature sections, and calm section pacing.
- Replaces menu items, prices, testimonials, staff, reservations, and cafe imagery with explicit owner-supplied placeholders.

### 15 A.D. Bakery / Custom static draft

- Uses the shared Instrument typography system with a restrained bakery palette, no external template code, and no decorative stock imagery.
