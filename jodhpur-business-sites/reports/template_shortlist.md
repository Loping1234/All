# Approved Template Shortlist

Research date: 2026-07-07  
Scope: 9 free templates from approved sources for three pitch-ready Jodhpur demo sites.  
Status: Research and license review only. No template files or code were downloaded, copied, or used to generate websites.

## Approval Guardrails

- All shortlisted templates are free and have clear first-party commercial/client adaptation terms.
- Tooplate templates may be adapted without attribution, but the original template package must not be redistributed as a template or collection.
- TemplateMo templates may be customized for clients and their credit links may be removed.
- Start Bootstrap free templates use the MIT license. No visible footer credit is required, but the copyright and license notice must remain with distributed source.
- Example menus, prices, products, reviews, testimonials, opening hours, staff, statistics, booking claims, social links, photos, and contact details must be removed unless verified. Use `[OWNER TO PROVIDE]` where required.
- Every eventual demo must display: “Unofficial demo website draft.”

- Template URL: https://www.framer.com/community/marketplace/templates/zest/

- Local-project constraint: For the existing static repository, either build the adapted end site in Framer or perform a manual licensed reimplementation after the user approves this direction. Do not represent the result as an original reusable template.

- Platform shown by source: Framer

### Other reviewed routes

- One Page Love's `Dann Good Coffee` is free and Webflow-cloneable, but it requires Webflow and a paid plan for a custom domain without the Webflow badge. It is less distinctive than Zest and is not approved for local static copying.
- Webflow returned multiple bakery templates, but the strongest bakery-specific results were paid. They were rejected because no template purchase was authorized.
- 21st.dev, Magic UI, Animate UI, Aceternity UI, React Bits, GSAP, and Codrops are useful component or motion sources, not complete bakery templates. Any future component must receive its own license review before use.
- Land-book, Lapa Ninja, Hoverstat.es, SiteInspire, and similar galleries are visual-reference sources only unless an individual item provides separate, explicit reuse rights.

### Recommendation for 15 A.D.

Use **Zest** as the structural template direction and **TAB Kitchen & Bakery** only as the visual-quality benchmark.

The adaptation should keep Zest's oversized editorial hero, compact vertical navigation idea, dark/light contrast, modular section rhythm, and subtle motion. It must remove the lemon identity, sample menu, products, reviews, FAQ answers, claims, and stock imagery. Until owner assets arrive, the hero should become a typography-led `15 A.D.` composition with clearly labeled `[OWNER TO PROVIDE]` media areas. The outlet-verification and owner-content sections from the current draft must remain.
